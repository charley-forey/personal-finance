# Index Fund Investing

## Why Index Funds
- Low expense ratios (often 0.03–0.20%)
- Broad diversification in a single fund
- Consistent with efficient market hypothesis

## Common Choices
- **US Total Market**: VTI, ITOT, SWTSX
- **International**: VXUS, IXUS
- **Bonds**: BND, AGG

## Three-Fund Portfolio
1. US stock index
2. International stock index
3. Bond index

Adjust stock/bond split based on age and risk tolerance (e.g., 120 minus age in stocks).

*Not a recommendation to buy specific securities.*
