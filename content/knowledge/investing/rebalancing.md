# Portfolio Rebalancing

## Why Rebalance
Markets move allocations away from targets. Rebalancing enforces "buy low, sell high" discipline.

## When to Rebalance
- **Threshold**: When any asset class drifts >5% from target
- **Calendar**: Annually or semi-annually
- **Hybrid**: Check quarterly, act only if threshold breached

## Tax Considerations
- Rebalance in tax-advantaged accounts first (no capital gains)
- In taxable accounts, use new contributions to adjust rather than selling
- Tax-loss harvest when selling winners in taxable accounts

## Example
Target 60/40 stocks/bonds. After a bull market, portfolio drifts to 70/30. Sell 10% stocks, buy bonds.
