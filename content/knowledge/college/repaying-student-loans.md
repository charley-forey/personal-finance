# Repaying Student Loans

## Know Your Loans
Federal vs private, interest rates, servicers, and repayment plans. Log in at StudentAid.gov for federal loan details.

## Repayment Plans
- **Standard**: Fixed payments over 10 years.
- **Income-driven**: Payments based on income; forgiveness after 20–25 years.
- **Refinancing**: Private refinance may lower rates but forfeits federal protections.

## Avalanche vs Snowball
**Avalanche**: Pay highest interest rate first — saves the most money. **Snowball**: Pay smallest balance first — builds momentum.

## Public Service Loan Forgiveness
Eligible federal borrowers in qualifying public service jobs may receive forgiveness after 120 qualifying payments under PSLF.

## Avoid Default
Enroll in income-driven repayment, request deferment/forbearance if needed, and communicate with your servicer before missing payments.
