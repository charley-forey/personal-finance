# Retirement Withdrawal Strategies

## The 4% Rule
Withdraw 4% of portfolio in year one, adjust for inflation annually. Historically supported 30-year retirements with 50/50 allocation.

## Dynamic Withdrawal
Reduce withdrawals in down markets (e.g., 4% becomes 3% when portfolio drops 20%). Preserves capital during sequence-of-returns risk.

## Bucket Strategy
- **Bucket 1**: 1–2 years cash for near-term expenses
- **Bucket 2**: 5–7 years bonds for medium-term
- **Bucket 3**: Stocks for long-term growth

## Tax-Efficient Order
Generally withdraw from taxable accounts first, then tax-deferred, then Roth last to maximize tax-deferred growth.
