# Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs)

## What They Are
Mandatory annual withdrawals from traditional IRAs and 401(k)s starting at age 73 (SECURE 2.0).

## How Calculated
Account balance on December 31 of prior year divided by IRS life expectancy factor from Uniform Lifetime Table.

## Penalty
Failing to take RMD: 25% excise tax on the shortfall (reduced to 10% if corrected within two years).

## Roth IRAs
No RMDs during owner's lifetime. Roth 401(k)s require RMDs unless rolled to Roth IRA.

## Planning Tips
- Take RMDs early in the year to avoid year-end rush
- Consider QCDs (qualified charitable distributions) from IRA after age 70½
