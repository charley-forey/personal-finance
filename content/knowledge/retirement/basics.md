# Retirement Planning Basics

## The 4% Rule
A common starting point: withdraw 4% of your portfolio annually in retirement, adjusted for inflation.

## Account Types
- **Traditional 401k/IRA**: Tax-deferred contributions, taxed on withdrawal
- **Roth 401k/IRA**: After-tax contributions, tax-free withdrawal
- **Taxable brokerage**: Flexible but less tax-efficient

## Social Security
Consider delaying benefits from 62 to 70 for higher monthly payments.
