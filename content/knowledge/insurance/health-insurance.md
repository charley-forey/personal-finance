# Health Insurance Planning

## Plan Types
- **HMO**: Lower cost, requires in-network providers and referrals.
- **PPO**: More flexibility, higher premiums and out-of-pocket costs.
- **HDHP + HSA**: High deductible with tax-advantaged health savings account — good for healthy households with emergency funds.

## Key Terms
- **Premium**: Monthly cost to maintain coverage.
- **Deductible**: Amount you pay before insurance kicks in.
- **Out-of-pocket max**: Annual ceiling on your share of costs.

## Open Enrollment
Review plans annually. Life changes (marriage, birth, job change) may qualify for special enrollment periods outside open enrollment.

## Cost Optimization
Use in-network providers, generic prescriptions, telehealth when appropriate, and compare procedure costs using insurer tools.
