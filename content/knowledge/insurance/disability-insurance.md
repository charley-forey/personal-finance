# Disability Insurance

## Purpose
Disability insurance replaces a portion of income if illness or injury prevents you from working. Your ability to earn is often your largest asset.

## Short-Term vs Long-Term
- **Short-term**: Covers weeks to months; often employer-provided.
- **Long-term**: Covers years until retirement age; critical for high earners and sole breadwinners.

## Coverage Target
Aim to replace 60–70% of take-home pay. Employer plans may cap benefits — supplement with individual coverage if needed.

## Own-Occupation vs Any-Occupation
**Own-occupation** policies pay if you cannot perform your specific job. **Any-occupation** only pays if you cannot work any job — less favorable for specialists.

## When to Buy
Purchase while healthy and employed. Premiums depend on age, occupation, health, and benefit period.
