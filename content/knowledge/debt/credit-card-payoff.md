# Credit Card Debt Payoff

## The Problem
Average credit card APR exceeds 20%. Minimum payments mostly cover interest, not principal.

## Strategy
1. Stop adding new charges
2. List all cards: balance, APR, minimum payment
3. Choose avalanche or snowball method
4. Pay as much extra as possible on target debt
5. Consider balance transfer to 0% APR card (watch transfer fees and promo period end)

## Balance Transfer Tips
- Fee is typically 3–5% of transferred amount
- Pay off before promotional rate expires
- Don't use the new card for purchases

## When to Consider Consolidation
Multiple high-rate cards with good credit may qualify for personal loan at lower fixed rate.
