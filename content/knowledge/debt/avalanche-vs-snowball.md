# Debt Avalanche vs Snowball

## Avalanche Method
Pay minimums on all debts, put extra toward the highest interest rate. Mathematically optimal — saves the most interest.

## Snowball Method
Pay minimums on all debts, put extra toward the smallest balance. Builds momentum with quick wins.

## Which to Choose
- **Avalanche**: If you're motivated by numbers and total savings
- **Snowball**: If you need psychological wins to stay consistent

## Example
Debts: $500 at 22%, $5,000 at 8%, $15,000 at 4%.
- Avalanche: Attack the 22% card first
- Snowball: Pay off the $500 card first

Both beat making only minimum payments.
