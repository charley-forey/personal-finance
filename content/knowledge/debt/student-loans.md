# Student Loan Repayment

## Federal Loan Types
- **Subsidized**: Government pays interest while in school
- **Unsubsidized**: Interest accrues immediately
- **PLUS**: Parent or graduate borrower loans

## Repayment Plans
- **Standard**: Fixed 10-year payoff
- **IDR (Income-Driven)**: PAYE, REPAYE, IBR — payment based on income, forgiveness after 20–25 years
- **PSLF**: Public Service Loan Forgiveness after 120 qualifying payments

## Refinancing
Private refinancing can lower rates for borrowers with strong credit and stable income. You lose federal protections (IDR, forgiveness, deferment).

## Extra Payment Strategy
Apply extra payments to highest-rate loan. Specify "apply to principal" in writing to servicer.

*Verify current federal program rules.*
