# Streaming Service Costs

## Average Spending
US households subscribe to 4+ streaming services, averaging $50–80/month.

## Cost Comparison (approximate)
- Netflix: $15–23/month
- Disney+: $8–14/month
- HBO Max: $16/month
- Spotify/Apple Music: $11/month
- YouTube Premium: $14/month

## Money-Saving Tactics
- Rotate services monthly (binge one, cancel, switch)
- Share family plans within household
- Use free tiers with ads where acceptable
- Check bundle deals (Disney/Hulu/ESPN, Amazon Prime add-ons)

## Annual vs Monthly
Annual plans often save 15–20%. Only commit if you'll use the service for the full year.

*Prices change frequently — verify current rates.*
