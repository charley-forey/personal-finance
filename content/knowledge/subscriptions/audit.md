# Subscription Audit

## Why Audit
The average household spends $200+/month on subscriptions. Many go unused.

## How to Audit
1. Review 3 months of bank/credit card statements
2. List every recurring charge with amount and frequency
3. Mark each: essential, nice-to-have, or unused
4. Cancel unused immediately
5. Negotiate or downgrade nice-to-have items

## Common Culprits
Streaming (multiple services), gym memberships, software trials that converted, cloud storage duplicates, meal kits, news paywalls.

## Prevention
- Use a dedicated virtual card for trials with spending limits
- Set calendar reminders before trial periods end
- Review subscriptions quarterly

## Savings Impact
Cutting $50/month = $600/year. Redirect to emergency fund or debt payoff.
