# Canceling Subscriptions

## Before Canceling
- Download any content you want to keep
- Note renewal date to avoid accidental charges
- Check if cancellation fee applies (rare for consumer services)

## How to Cancel
- **App Store/Google Play**: Cancel through store settings, not just delete the app
- **Direct billing**: Cancel through provider website; keep confirmation email
- **Credit card**: Update card number as last resort if provider makes cancellation difficult

## After Canceling
- Verify no charge appears next billing cycle
- Watch for "win-back" offers — sometimes 50% off for 3 months
- Only re-subscribe if you'll actively use it

## Legal Protections
FTC Click-to-Cancel rule requires cancellation as easy as sign-up for many services.
