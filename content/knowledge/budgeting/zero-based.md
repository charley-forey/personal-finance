# Zero-Based Budgeting

## How It Works
Every dollar of income is assigned a job before the month begins. Income minus allocations equals zero.

## Steps
1. List monthly take-home income
2. List fixed expenses (rent, insurance, debt minimums)
3. Allocate variable categories (groceries, dining, entertainment)
4. Assign remaining to savings, debt payoff, or sinking funds
5. Track actual spending against allocations weekly

## Benefits
- Eliminates "where did my money go?"
- Forces prioritization of goals
- Works well with irregular income when using last month's income

## Tools
Envelope method (cash), spreadsheet, or budgeting apps with category tracking.
