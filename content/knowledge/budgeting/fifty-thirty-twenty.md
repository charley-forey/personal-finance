# 50/30/20 Budget Rule

## The Split
- **50% Needs**: Housing, utilities, groceries, insurance, minimum debt payments, transportation
- **30% Wants**: Dining out, entertainment, hobbies, non-essential shopping
- **20% Savings & Debt**: Emergency fund, retirement, extra debt payments

## Example
$5,000 monthly take-home:
- Needs: $2,500
- Wants: $1,500
- Savings/Debt: $1,000

## Adjustments
High cost-of-living areas may push needs above 50%. Temporarily acceptable — adjust wants and savings proportionally.

## Getting Started
1. Calculate actual spending in each bucket for 2–3 months
2. Identify the biggest gap vs targets
3. Cut wants first, then optimize needs (refinance, negotiate bills)

*A starting framework, not a rigid rule.*
