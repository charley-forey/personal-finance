# Emergency Fund Guidelines

## How Much
- **Starter**: $1,000 while paying high-interest debt
- **Standard**: 3–6 months of essential expenses
- **Conservative**: 6–12 months if variable income or single income household

## What Counts as Essential
Housing, utilities, food, insurance, minimum debt payments, transportation. Exclude dining out, subscriptions, entertainment.

## Where to Keep It
High-yield savings account (HYSA) — liquid, FDIC insured, earns 4–5% APY. Not invested in stocks (volatility risk).

## When to Use It
True emergencies only: job loss, medical crisis, urgent home repair. Not vacations or planned purchases.

## Rebuilding
After using emergency funds, pause discretionary spending until replenished to target level.
