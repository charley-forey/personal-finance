# Retirement Account Contribution Limits

## 2024 Limits (approximate)
- **401(k)**: $23,000 employee deferral; $69,000 total with employer match
- **IRA/Roth IRA**: $7,000 ($8,000 if age 50+)
- **HSA**: $4,150 individual / $8,300 family

## Traditional vs Roth
- **Traditional**: Deduct now, pay tax on withdrawal
- **Roth**: Pay tax now, tax-free growth and withdrawals

## Roth Conversion Strategy
Convert during lower-income years (career change, sabbatical) to fill lower tax brackets. Consider state taxes if relocating.

## Catch-Up Contributions
Age 50+ can add extra $7,500 to 401(k) and $1,000 to IRA.

*Limits change annually — verify current IRS guidance.*
