# End of Year Tax Checklist

## Documents to Gather
- W-2 from all employers
- 1099 forms (interest, dividends, freelance)
- Mortgage interest statement (1098)
- Student loan interest
- HSA/FSA contributions
- Charitable donation receipts

## Estimated Tax Review
Review your YTD withholding against estimated liability. If you owe more than $1,000, you may need quarterly estimated payments.

## Capital Gains
Review realized gains/losses in your investment accounts. Consider tax-loss harvesting before year end.

*This is informational only — consult a CPA for tax filing.*
