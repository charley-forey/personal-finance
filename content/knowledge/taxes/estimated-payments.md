# Estimated Tax Payments

## Who Needs Them
Self-employed individuals, freelancers, and anyone with significant income not subject to withholding (investment income, rental income) may need quarterly estimated payments.

## Safe Harbor Rules
You generally avoid penalties if you pay at least:
- 90% of current year tax liability, or
- 100% of prior year liability (110% if AGI exceeds $150,000)

## Due Dates
- Q1: April 15
- Q2: June 15
- Q3: September 15
- Q4: January 15 (following year)

## How to Pay
Use IRS Direct Pay or EFTPS. Keep records of payment dates and amounts.

*Informational only — consult a CPA for your specific situation.*
