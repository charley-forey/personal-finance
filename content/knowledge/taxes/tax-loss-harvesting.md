# Tax-Loss Harvesting

## What It Is
Selling investments at a loss to offset capital gains and up to $3,000 of ordinary income per year.

## Wash Sale Rule
You cannot claim a loss if you buy a "substantially identical" security within 30 days before or after the sale. This includes purchases in IRAs and spouse's accounts.

## Best Practices
- Review taxable accounts in November–December
- Pair losses against realized gains first
- Consider replacing sold positions with similar (not identical) ETFs
- Track carryforward losses on Schedule D

## When It Helps Most
High-income years with large capital gains, or when rebalancing taxable portfolios.
