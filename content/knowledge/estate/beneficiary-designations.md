# Beneficiary Designations

## Why They Matter
Beneficiary forms on retirement accounts, life insurance, and bank accounts override your will. Incorrect or outdated designations are a common estate planning mistake.

## Primary vs Contingent
**Primary** beneficiaries receive assets first. **Contingent** beneficiaries inherit if primaries predecease you.

## Per Stirpes vs Per Capita
- **Per stirpes**: A deceased beneficiary's share passes to their descendants.
- **Per capita**: Shares split equally among surviving beneficiaries at the same level.

## Review Schedule
Check beneficiaries after every major life event and at least annually. Divorce does not automatically remove an ex-spouse.

## Minors
Naming minor children directly can trigger court guardianship. Consider a trust or UTMA/UGMA with professional guidance.
